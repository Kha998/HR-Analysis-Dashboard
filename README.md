# HR Analytics Dashboard ‚Äì Attrition, Demographics, Salary & Performance

This project presents an **interactive HR analytics dashboard built using Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, and salary‚Äìperformance alignment. The goal is to support **data-driven HR decision-making** by identifying retention risks, understanding workforce composition, and evaluating compensation effectiveness.

---

## Project Objective
To develop a comprehensive HR analytics solution that helps organizations:
- Identify high-risk departments and job roles for attrition  
- Understand workforce demographic patterns  
- Assess whether compensation aligns with employee performance  



## Project Overview
The analysis is based on **1,470 employee records** and is delivered through **three integrated Power BI dashboards**, supported by insight and recommendation pages:

- **Attrition Analysis Dashboard**  
  Analyzes attrition rate, turnover trends, high-risk departments and job roles, and key drivers such as overtime and job satisfaction.

- **Employee Demographic Analysis Dashboard**  
  Examines workforce distribution by age, gender, tenure, department, experience, and business travel.

- **Salary & Performance Analysis Dashboard**  
  Evaluates salary distribution, performance ratings, high-performer proportion, and pay‚Äìperformance alignment.

## Key Business Questions
- What is the overall attrition rate, and which departments and job roles are most affected?
- What factors are associated with higher employee turnover?
- How is the workforce distributed across age, gender, tenure, and departments?
- Which employee segments present the highest retention risk?
- Does higher compensation consistently align with higher performance?

---

##  Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is **16%**, concentrated in **Research & Development and Sales**.
- Attrition is strongly linked to **role-specific pressure and sustained overtime**.
- The workforce is dominated by **mid-career employees (ages 26‚Äì45)** with an average tenure of **7 years**.
- Only **15% of employees** are classified as high performers.
- Compensation is **not always aligned with performance**, particularly in Sales roles.

---

##  Recommendations
- Apply **targeted retention strategies** in high-risk departments and roles.
- Address **workload and overtime-related risks** to reduce burnout.
- Strengthen **mid-career employee development and retention programs**.
- Improve **pay-for-performance alignment** through clearer reward policies.
- Use HR analytics dashboards for **continuous monitoring and decision-making**.
